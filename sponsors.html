Online Engagement

Social Media - #UGAHacks


Your company can engage with us on social media by using the hashtag #UGAHacks or tagging us @UGAHacks on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. We'll share photos and post taken throughout the event.


Slack


Each sponsor has access to their own slack channel. Slack is an excellent opportunity to engage with participants outside of workshop times or before/after they visit your booth. You can use the '@channel' tag to send a notification to everyone in your channel. By default, all participants will automatically be enrolled in your specific channel.

On Site Engagement

Table Cloth

Company branded table cloths can help make your table stand out from the other tables, giving your brand a more unique look.


Banners

If you have any vertical banners, feel free to bring them to showcase what your company is all about.


Recruiting

If you plan on recruiting at the event, be sure to have brochures or flyers detailing your program because let's face it, college students tend to forget things quite often.


Swag

Giving out promotional items for participants is a great way to increase engagement and encourage students to take a look at what your table has to offer. Common swag items include t-shirts, fidget spinners, flash lights, and stickers. Feel free to get creative!

Workshop Suggestions

Know your audience

Determine whether you want to target advanced or beginner/intermediate. Focusing your efforts towards one group can greatly improve the quality of your workshop by not jumping back and forth, confusing both groups. If you would like advice, please feel free to reach out to us.


Give them shoes and let them run

Unfortunately, although the 48 hours will be fun and fruitful, it usually isn't enough time for us to include everything there is to learn about the subject you'll be speaking about, we recommend laying down the basics during the workshop and providing supplementary materials that participants can view on their own after or on their own time!


Visual Aids are helpful

You will have access to a projector with either an HDMI or VGA connection.


Let participants know ahead of time

Be sure to specify any materials (including software to download) that participants should come with -- download times can really hinder workshop time!


Relax and have fun

Have fun, and feel free to reach out the team if you need any further assistance or feedback -- we would love to help!
