about

Sowing the seed for the next generation of coders.

Come join UGA for our 4th annual Hackathon!


Hackathons are all about a group of dedicated people coming together at a workspace to create something awesome. The 36 hour hackathon is free and open to anyone.


UGAHacks includes mentors, free food, video game competitions, workshops, and more. There's even mixers at the event for finding teammates!

challenges

Don't know what to code? Don't worry - challenges have your back.


Challenges are projects geared around using a specific theme or tool. The best project built around each challenge will receive a prize.


Come find out what challenges we'll anounce that we and our sponsors have in store for you!

FAQ

Where?

LeakyHacks, University of Georgia, Athens, GA

Who can sign up?

All students! We welcome all undergraduates and graduate students to attend. Professionals and other guests are welcome to attend as mentors or volunteers.

How much will it cost to sign up?

Nothing! The entire event and its amazing perks are free for all participants, including meals and snacks to keep you powered throughout the weekend.

Can I volunteer / mentor at UGAHacks?

We love your enthusiasm! Volunteers and mentors can apply via contacting us at hackathon@uga.edu - Be sure to mention it in the subject. See you soon!

I have more questions?

That's not actually a question, but if you've got one then we'll be happy to answer it! Reach out to us at hello@ugahacks.com
