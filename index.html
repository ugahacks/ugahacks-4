    about

    Sowing the seed for the next generation of coders.

    Come join UGA for our 4th annual Hackathon!

    This year, we encourage you to think outside the box. It's okay to have disorganized, inefficient code. It is a natural process of improving as a coder.

    Hackathons are all about a group of dedicated people coming together at a workspace to create something awesome. The 36 hour hackathon is free and open to anyone.

    It includes mentors, free food, video game competitions, workshops, and more. There's even mixers at the event for anyone that still needs to find some teammates!

    challenges

    Don't know what to code? Don't worry - challenges have your back.

    Challenges are projects where we have clearly defined the rules and there is a definite end goal. Once completed, you can cash them in for prizes.

    It's first come first serve, so time is of the essence!
